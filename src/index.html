INFOSEC KIDS IS
a community for kids and parents for teaching their kids how to be safe and secure online.

OUR AIM
Helping kids on how to make responsible decision about cyber security.
HOW WE ENCOURAGE KIDS
We conduct our kid-friendly sessions to help them learn more about protecting their identity online.
HOW YOU CAN HELP
Collaborate with us and help kids understand how to stay safe online.



WHY INFOSEC KIDS

  • We've curated some of the best cybersecurity resources available for kids of every age to keep them entertained, educated, and safe while they spend more time on the internet.
  • Creating a comfortable atmosphere where kids can speak their minds and stay curious.
  • Making them stay up to date with the latest trends and innovations in the field of security.





OUR AIM

  • Helping kids remember that cyber safety should always be a priority and help to ensure younger children know the basics of staying safe online.
  • Helping kids how to make responsible decisions about online communication and learn about key issues of digital citizenship.
  • Helping your kids stay safe and healthy while growing up in the Digital Age.
  • Helping and teaching kids how to identify bullying and how to stand up to it safely.





    HOW YOU CAN HELP

  • Collaborate with us and help them understand how to stay safe online.
  • Make online security one of the most essential lesson to be imparted to your kids.
  • Make your kids engage in our kid-friendly sessions to learn more about protecting your identity online.
